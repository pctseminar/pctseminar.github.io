Pacific Category Theory Seminar

The Pacific Category Theory (PCT) seminar is an online seminar for the category theory community in the Asia-Pacific time zone and beyond. We welcome talks in all areas of pure and applied category theory in relationship with other mathematical areas, computer science, etc.

It will run once or twice a month on Friday at 10am JST/12pm AED (1am UTC). The zoom link will be posted on the webpage shortly before.
